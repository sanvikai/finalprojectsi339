Welcome to Universal Studios Hollywood!

Universal Studios Hollywood is unique in the fact that it is a theme park that is an operating film studio. It is located in Los Angeles County in Universal City, which is a county island. The park was opened as a movie studio in 1912 and as a theme park in 1964. It is known for its studio tour which lets visitors see real sets where their favorite movies are filmed. It is also home to the Wizarding World of Harry Potter which brings in fans from all around the world.

My Experience!

I loved my time at Universal Studios Hollywood. The main pull to Universal is thier shows. In addition to the studio tour, I remember watching a live water show, a special effects show, and an animal actors show. This live entertainment is where Universal sets itself apart.

My Top 3 Rides

  • #1 Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey™: "Make your way through the classrooms and corridors of Hogwarts™."
  • #2 TRANSFORMERS™: The Ride-3D: "Fusing photo-realistic 3D HD media and elaborate flight simulation technology with cutting edge physical and special effects."
  • #3 Despicable Me Minion Mayhem: "Join Gru, his daughters, and the mischievous Minions on a heartwarming and hilarious ride. "
