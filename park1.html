Welcome to The Disneyland Resort!

The Disneyland Resort is located in Anaheim, California, a suburb of Los Angeles. The resort is made up of two theme parks: Disneyland and Disney California Adventure (My Personal Favorite). It also encompasses Downtown Disney which is essentially an outdoor mall with shopping and dining options. Also on site, are three Disney themed hotels (Disneyland Hotel, Disneyâ€™s Grand Californian, and the Pixar Place Hotel). The park was designed by Walt Disney himself and was opened in 1955, making the park 69 years old!

My Experience!

This is my personal favorite theme park ever. Both parks at the Disneyland resort are amazing! My unpopular opinion is that I prefer the Disneyland Resort to Walt DisneyWorld because of how compact it is, making all of the attractions more attainable to visit in one trip. Additionally, it is clear that Disneyland is the original because of some of its unique touches such as the apartment where Walt Disney lived being above the fire station on Main St.

My Top 3 Rides

  • #1 Radiator Springs Racers: "Start your engines! Zoom through the desert landscape of Cars Land, inspired by the Disney and Pixar movie Cars."
  • #2 Matterhorn Bobsleds: "Hurtle through a snow-capped mountain on a speeding alpine sled while avoiding the clutches of the Abominable Snowman."
  • #3 Grizzly River Run: "Embark on a thrilling whitewater raft adventure that splashes you down a roaring California river deep in the mountains."
