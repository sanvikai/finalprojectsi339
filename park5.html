Welcome to Walt Disney World!

Disney World is a resort located in Orlando, FL. It opened in 1971, making the resort over fifty years old. There are many attractions in the resort. It is made up of four theme parks (Magic Kingdom, Epcot, Hollywood Studios, Animal Kingdom), 2 water parks (Typhoon Lagoon, Blizzard Beach), as well as other attractions like Disney Springs and ESPN's Wide World of Sports Complex.

My Experience!

I've only been here once when I was a very young child, but going here at such a young age made me fall in lvoe with all things amusement/theme parks. The resort itself is massive, and you can truly never run out of htings to do here. Additionally, Disney owns so much land around Disney World so they can expand the attractions for a long time and keep on innovating.

  • #1 Seven Dwarfs Mine Train: "Race through the diamond mine from Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs on a swaying family coaster ride."
  • #2 Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance: "Join the Resistance in an unforgettable battle against the First Order on this exciting ride."
  • #3 Kali River Rapids: "Set out on a thrilling whitewater adventure through a lush jungle in the heart of Asia."
