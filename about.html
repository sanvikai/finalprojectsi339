Hi, I'm Sanvika!

Welcome to my theme park review website! I'm passionate about theme parks. I not only love the thrilling rides, I also love the themes, the food, and the other attractions that theme parks have to offer.

A little bit about me: I'm currently a sophomore at the University of Michigan, planning on studying UX Design and Business.

My dream career when I was younger was to create a theme park. I now see how UX can help me with that. Design skills will help me design the layout of the park, research skills will help identify oppurtunities, and learning accessibility can help ensure that the theme park, and its digital products are accessible to all.
