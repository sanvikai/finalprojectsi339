Welcome to Silver Dollar City!

Silver Dollar City is a famous theme park located in Branson, MO. It opened back in May of 1960, over sixty years ago. It is located in the Ozarks and has 2.2 million visitors every year. Silver Dollar City is themed according to the 1880s. It has 31 attractions total, with seven big roller coasters. One unique feature is that Silver Dollar City is located near Marvel Cave, a big attration in the Ozarks. Visitors can go on the cave tour before heading to the park.

My Experience!

Visiting Silver Dollar City and The Ozarks is a tradition well known for any kid that grew up in the midwest. Silver Dollar City is great for those who are looking for thrills. My only pro tip is to not go on the Time Travler if you are prone to any sort of motion sickness :(

My Top 3 Rides

  • #1 Thunderation: "Rumble through the treetops on a runaway mine train!"
  • #2 Fire in the Hole: "Get ready to ride the ALL-NEW record-setting indoor roller coaster through the darkness to save the burning town of Marmaros from the notorious baldknobbers!"
  • #3 WildFire: "A high-flying, multi-looping, cobra-rolling coaster that will leave you breathless!"
