Welcome to Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk!

The Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk is an amusement park that is oceanfront in Northern California. It is California's oldest amusement park. The park has 35 attrcations, which includes 3 roller coasters. The park opened in 1907, that is over a century ago! Visitors don't even need a fee to get in, they can head to the beach in relax. If visitors so choose, they can play the games, eat the food, and ride the rides that the boardwalk has to offer.

My Experience!

I have been to the boardwalk three times at three vastly differnt times of my life. I've always went when visiting my aunt and uncle in the Bay Area. I've been at ages 7, 11, and 15. Each time I went, I found a new thing to be excited about. Even as someone who doesn't particulary enjoy the beach, having the boardwalk made it the perfect place to go with family, so those who do want to stay on the beach can, but those who want to go on rides can also do what they want. Overall, the Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk truly does have something for everybody.
